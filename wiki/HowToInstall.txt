= How to Install =

If you use RPM file, you just need to download rpm file in attach part and launch 

{{{
rpm -ivh xmlBuilderPlugin-1.0-1.noarch.rpm
}}}


If you get souce from svn, you should go in your .gimp-2.4/plug-ins and launch:

{{{
svn checkout http://svn2.assembla.com/svn/xmlBuilderPlugin/trunk 
}}}
